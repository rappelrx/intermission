Join

Whether you're an instrumentalist, singer, arranger, artist, videographer, photographer, audio engineer...We've got a place for you!

If you're interested in getting involved in Intermission, just shoot us a message on social media or email us at IntermissionUCSD@gmail.com.

Orchestra and Choir

We welcome players of all instruments and singers to join! Whether you're a beginner or an expert, we'd love to have you be part of our Intermission family. And even if you play an instrument that might not be part of a typical orchestra's instrumentation, we arrange our own music — so we can arrange to include your instrument. Let's play our favorite music, together!

Tech Team

We record our own performances. Currently, we have a small team of photographers, videographers, and an audio engineer to document our concerts and rehearsals. If you have some experience with photography, videography, video editing, or audio, we can always use more help with Tech Team!

Art Committee

During our second year, we started realizing that not only are our members musically talented, some of them are artistically talented as well. So, we created an internal Art Committee to create art for promoting our upcoming pieces. Throughout the quarter, we post these artworks to reveal pieces that people can anticipate for the concert. Have some artistic talent? Join Art Committee!

Contact us!

Interested in joining the orchestra, choir, Tech Team, or Art Committee? Contact us via email, intermissionUCSD@gmail.com, or message us on social media.
